Privacy Policy — Heartpress

Effective date: 2026-01-01
App: Heartpress
Developer: Technopresso IT Solutions

This Privacy Policy explains how Technopresso IT Solutions (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use Heartpress (the “App”).

1) Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information generated through your use of the App.

A. Information you provide

  • Account and profile: name, email address, age/date of birth (or age), gender, photos, bio, interests.
  • Content and communications: messages and other content you create or share through the App.
  • Support: information you provide when contacting us for support or reporting issues.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Approximate location: used to help show nearby matches (if you enable location permissions).
  • Usage data: interactions with features (e.g., swipes/likes), app activity, and performance/diagnostic data.
  • Device data: device identifiers and technical info needed for app functionality and security.

Note: “Approximate location” may be derived from device signals and may not represent precise GPS coordinates.

2) How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the service (create accounts, enable matching, and deliver chat features).
  • Maintain safety and security (fraud prevention, abuse detection, enforcing our policies).
  • Improve the App (debugging, analytics, and feature optimization).
  • Communicate with you (service messages, updates, and support responses).

3) How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in the following limited situations:

  • With other users: your profile information is shown to other users in the App as part of the matching experience.
  • Service providers: trusted vendors who help operate the App (e.g., hosting, analytics, customer support), under contractual obligations to protect data.
  • Legal and safety reasons: when required by law, or to protect users, prevent harm, investigate fraud/abuse, or enforce our terms and policies.
  • Business changes: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to applicable law.

4) Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to help protect your information, such as:

  • Encryption in transit (e.g., HTTPS/TLS).
  • Access controls and restricted permissions for internal systems.
  • Monitoring and security practices designed to reduce unauthorized access.

No system can be 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

5) Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or update your information.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Request an export of certain data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent (e.g., location permission in your device settings).
